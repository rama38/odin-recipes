Banana Smoothie Recipe

Image of Banana Smoothie

Low fat, no-sugar-added milk banana smoothie.

Recipe by: M131984 @allrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 ½ bananas
  • 5 (1 gram) packets low calorie granulated sugar substitute (such as Sweet 'n Low®)

Steps

  1. Blend milk, bananas, and sugar substitute in a blender or food processor until smooth.
